# Acknowledgments

Brain Researcher builds on open scientific data standards, neuroimaging tools,
knowledge resources, language-model infrastructure, and open-source service
software. We are grateful to the maintainers, dataset stewards, standards
groups, and research communities whose work makes auditable AI-assisted
neuroimaging infrastructure possible.

This page is an attribution and appreciation list. Inclusion does not imply
endorsement by the named projects, institutions, companies, or dataset
providers. Dataset-specific licenses, data-use agreements, citation rules, and
redistribution limits remain with the upstream providers.

## Data Boundary Note

The public Brain Researcher repository ships code, contracts, docs, service
templates, and public-safe helper scripts. It does not ship private benchmark
corpora, compiled Neo4j graph dumps, private dataset mounts, internal run
artifacts, or site-specific launchers. Any use of external datasets must follow
the upstream provider's access terms and citation requirements.
